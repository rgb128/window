# Window by Creative Association Grayness

**Window** is an interactive art piece that fragments multiple images into a single, cohesive grid. Every triangle and quadrilateral you see is a "window" into a different high-resolution photograph, all aligned to form a perfect square in the center of your screen.

When you click and hold (or touch the screen), the grid reacts to your position. Points push away from your finger, causing the shapes near your touch to expand while surrounding figures shrink toward the edges. This distortion continues until the selected area fills the majority of the viewport.

Releasing your hold triggers an instant reset. The system discards the current view and generates a completely new layout with a fresh set of images and randomized geometry. The experience is powered by an automated background cache that ensures new "windows" are always ready to be viewed without delay
